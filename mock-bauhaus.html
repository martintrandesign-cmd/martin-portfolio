Beyond the flows, I built internal tooling: a no-code Figma bot builder the team still uses, and an LLM-powered Co-pilot that turns support transcripts into AI skill specs in minutes instead of weeks.
You
Tell me more about the Co-pilot tool
Martin
The Co-pilot analyzes raw support transcripts, scores intents by automation potential, and generates a complete AI skill spec — NLU model, flows, edge cases, escalation logic. I built it with Cursor and Claude. What used to take a designer days or weeks now takes minutes. It's live — you can try it from the Work section.
